Serving hospitals, clinics, and health centers across the Dessie region.

Geographic Coverage

Dessie branch of the Ethiopian Pharmaceuticals Supply Service (EPSS) covers public health facilities in the South Wollo Zone and the Dessie City Administration.


Primary Service Area:


The branch is one of the main pharmaceutical hubs and constantly supplies healthcare facilities, including 14 hospitals and 141 health centers, within the 20 districts of the South Wollo Zone and the Dessie City Administration.

Operational Definition:


Generally, EPSS branches aim to cover health facilities that are reachable within 13 hours of driving time and/or within 160 to 300 kilometers from the branch warehouse. Facilities outside this benchmark are considered beyond the immediate coverage area.

Regional Network:


The Dessie branch is part of the larger EPSS network, which has 19 branches across Ethiopia, and serves public health facilities within the Amhara Region alongside the Addis Ababa 2, Bahir Dar, and Gondar branches.

Accessibility:


Delivery of pharmaceuticals is primarily provided to woreda health offices and health facilities located along major roads.
