Core Services

📦 Medicine Distribution

Ethiopian Pharmaceuticals Supply Service (EPSS) Dessie Branch operates a sophisticated distribution network to provide medicine to its catchment area, which currently encompasses over 90 health facilities across the eastern and southern zones of the Amhara Region

🏭 Warehousing

Ethiopian Pharmaceuticals Supply Service (EPSS) maintains standardized warehousing operations across its 19 branches, including Dessie, to ensure compliance with Good Storage Practices (GSP). These practices are regulated by the Ethiopian Food and Drug Authority (EFDA) to preserve the safety and efficacy of medical supplies.

✅ Quality Assurance

Ethiopian Pharmaceuticals Supply Service (EPSS) operates a robust quality assurance (QA) framework in coordination with the Ethiopian Food and Drug Authority (EFDA). This system ensures that every medicine distributed via hubs like Dessie meets strict safety and efficacy standards before reaching patients.
