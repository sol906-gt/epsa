Meet Our 45+ Professionals

👨‍💼 Getachew Mekonnen

Getachew Mekonnen is the Manager of the Ethiopian Pharmaceuticals Supply Service (EPSS) Dessie Branch. Under his leadership, the branch serves as a critical pharmaceutical hub for the Amhara Region, overseeing the warehousing and distribution of essential medicines to over 90 health facilities.

👩‍⚕️ Selamawit Alemu

Selamawit Alemu serves as a senior pharmaceutical professional within the Ethiopian healthcare sector. While public records often highlight her as a clinical pharmacist and researcher, her role at the Ethiopian Pharmaceuticals Supply Service (EPSS) Dessie Branch is focused on technical leadership in pharmacy operations and supply chain integrity.

🏭 Tesfaye Hailu

Tesfaye Hailu serves as the Warehouse Manager for the Ethiopian Pharmaceuticals Supply Service (EPSS) Dessie Branch. He is a critical member of the branch’s leadership team, working alongside Branch Manager Getachew Mekonnen to ensure the integrity of the medical supply chain in the Amhara Region
